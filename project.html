Roku: A Streaming Platform

This is a remake of the tv streaming platform known as Roku, connecting streaming channels, such as Youtube, Neflix, Sky News, Crave and live sports on subscription channels, inlcuding NBA and NHL. This project is focused remaking the UX/UI aspect of the platform for a more user friendly built-in streaming experience.

The intent is to modernize this older platform, that has become outdated visually, and update it for a new audience.
